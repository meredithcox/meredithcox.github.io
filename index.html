Projects

Starfruit Apparel
I built an online store in response to a problem: A clothing aesthetic that I enjoy is usually sold cheaply and made with questionable labor practices. I decided to create a store that pairs a unique and minimalist look with ethical practices. Having no formal design or business training, I learned on the spot how to use Photoshop, how to build a site showcasing the brand's essential look, and how to use social media to reach my target audience and learn more about what my customers want.
